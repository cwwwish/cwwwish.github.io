About Me

My Skills

As an electrician technician, my main area of expertise lies in designing, installing, and maintaining electrical systems, including wiring, circuit breakers, and energy-efficient solutions. I have significant experience troubleshooting complex electrical issues using tools like multimeters, oscilloscopes, and PLCs. My work with the Department of Defense (DoD) has honed my ability to interpret blueprints and ensure compliance with strict safety standards, such as NEC and OSHA, allowing me to deliver reliable systems in high-stakes environments. My passion for electrical technology drives me to stay current with advancements in the field, including micro grids and renewable energy solutions. I thrive in collaborative settings, enjoying partnerships with professionals to deliver innovative, high-quality electrical solutions. In addition to my technical expertise, I pride myself on my strong communication skills, meticulous attention to detail, and creative problem-solving abilities. I am committed to advancing my expertise in electrical technology and look forward to continuing to grow not only as an electrician but also as a leader.
